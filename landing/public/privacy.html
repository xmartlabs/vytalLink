Privacy Policy

Your health data privacy is our top priority

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

At vytalLink, we believe your health data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Key principle: Your health data stays on your device. We only access it when you explicitly request AI insights through our secure, encrypted connections.

Information We Collect

Health Data

  • Device Storage: All health data (steps, heart rate, sleep, workouts) is stored locally on your device
  • Temporary Access: We only access your health data when you make a specific request through an AI assistant
  • No Storage: We do not store your health data on our servers

Authentication Data

  • Connection codes and session tokens for AI integration
  • Encrypted authentication keys (stored temporarily)
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)

Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and crash reports
  • Network connectivity information

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Integration: Securely transmit requested health data to your chosen AI assistant
  • Authentication: Verify connections between the app and AI services
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality
  • Support: Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues

Data Sharing and Third Parties

AI Services

When you request health insights, we securely transmit only the specific data you've requested to your chosen AI service (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.). This data is sent encrypted and is not stored by vytalLink.

Third-Party Services

  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data may be shared with analytics services
  • Crash Reporting: Technical crash data to improve app stability
  • No Health Data: We never share your health data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes

Security Measures

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted
  • Local Storage: Health data remains on your device
  • Secure Authentication: OAuth 2.0 and secure token management
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • No Persistent Storage: Session data is cleared after use

Your Rights and Control

  • Data Access: View all data stored by the app
  • Data Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Selective Sharing: Choose exactly what health data to share
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect AI services at any time
  • Export Data: Download your data in a portable format

Data Retention

  • Health Data: Never stored on our servers
  • Session Data: Cleared immediately after AI requests
  • Authentication Tokens: Expired and deleted after 24 hours
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data retained for 12 months

Children's Privacy

vytalLink is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

vytalLink is designed to comply with international privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA principles. Users in the European Union have additional rights under GDPR.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
