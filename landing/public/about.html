About vytalLink

Bridging the gap between your health data and artificial intelligence

Our Mission

At vytalLink, we believe that your health data should work for you. Our mission is to make personal health information accessible to AI assistants in a secure, private, and user-controlled way.

We're democratizing access to health insights by connecting your fitness trackers, health apps, and wearable devices with the most advanced AI assistants available today.

How It Started

vytalLink was born from a simple frustration: having tons of health data trapped in different apps and devices, but no easy way to get meaningful insights from it.

We realized that AI assistants like ChatGPT and Claude could provide incredible health insights, but they needed access to our personal data in a secure way. That's where vytalLink comes in.

Our Values

Privacy First

Your health data stays on your device. We only provide secure access when you explicitly request it.

User Control

You decide what data to share, when to share it, and which AI assistants can access it.

Open Innovation

We believe in open standards like MCP to ensure interoperability across AI platforms.
