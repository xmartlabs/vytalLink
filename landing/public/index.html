Two ways to use vytalLink

Whether you want health insights for yourself or you're building a product, the mechanism is the same: the app holds your data, AI reads it through a secure connection.

Personal health AI

Use ChatGPT or Claude to ask questions about your own data.

  1. 1
    Download vytalLink

    Get the app on iOS or Android. It reads your health data from Apple Health or Health Connect.

  2. 2
    Tap Get Word + PIN

    The app generates a short word and a 6-digit PIN. These are your temporary access codes for the session.

  3. 3
    Open ChatGPT or Claude

    Find the vytalLink GPT, or set up Claude Desktop. Paste your Word + PIN when asked.

  4. 4
    Ask your questions

    Keep the app open and start chatting. Your AI reads your data in real time.

See setup guides

Developer integration

Add health data to your own agent. Your users connect through the app, you don't need to build anything mobile.

  1. 1
    Install the MCP server

    One npm package. Works with any MCP-compatible agent in TypeScript or Python.

  2. 2
    Send users to vytalLink

    Point them to vytallink.xmartlabs.com/app. They download the app and get a Word + PIN.

  3. 3
    Call direct_login in your agent

    Your agent accepts their Word + PIN and authenticates. No browser redirect, no OAuth dance.

  4. 4
    Query health data

    Use get_summary or get_health_metrics to pull steps, sleep, heart rate, and more.
